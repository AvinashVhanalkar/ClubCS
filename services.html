Club SC Services

HIIT Exercise Classes

HIIT Exercise classes can be very beneficial for cardio workouts. They can help improve cardiovascular health, increase endurance and burn calories.This training method is known for its effectiveness in burning calories, improving cardiovascular fitness, and boosting overall endurance.

Nutrition Consulting

Nutrition consulting involves seeking guidance and advice from qualified professionals to optimize one's diet and make informed choices regarding food and nutrition.It can help people develop a balanced and make informed decisions about their nutrition that meets their specific needs and goals.

Personal Training

Personal training is a fitness service that provides one-on-one coaching and guidance for individuals who want to achieve their fitness goals. Personal training involves working with a certified fitness professional on a one-on-one basis to achieve specific health and fitness goals.

Private lessons

Private lessons for individuals who want more personalized attention and instruction. They can also be a good option for individuals who are recovering from an injury or have specific fitness goals that require more individualized attention.

Weight Training

It's important to work with a professional trainer or instructor to learn proper form and technique,as well as to develop a safe and effective weight training program that's tailored to your individual fitness goals and needs. They evaluate clients current fitness levels.
